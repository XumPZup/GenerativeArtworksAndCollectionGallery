Arcangels

This piece is an exploration of Bezier curves, circular repetion and simmetry. All shapes are generated by two or more curves and then repeted in a circular way. Everything about this piece is random e.g. color, position and shape of the curves and number of repetitions.

SupaShape

This piece was made using six supershapes functions connected to one another. Every supershape is randomized and it's parameters are changign over time and the way they are connected to one another also changes over time.

Bezier Garden

This piece was made using quadratic and Bezier curves to make up a random generated garden. This is the source of the Bezier Garden collection.
